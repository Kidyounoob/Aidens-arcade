FAQ

Quick answers.

Can I put Netflix inside my site?

Usually no — Netflix blocks embedding in iframes. YouTube embeds are the normal approach.

Why does YouTube sometimes not load?

If the network/device blocks YouTube, the embed is blocked too.

Does it remember my theme?

Yes — theme + dark/light are saved in localStorage.
