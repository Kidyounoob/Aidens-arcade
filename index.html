FAQ

Quick answers.

Can I put Netflix inside my site?

Usually no — Netflix blocks being embedded in iframes. YouTube embeds are the normal way.

Will this unblock YouTube at school?

No. If YouTube is blocked, embeds are blocked too. This is just a UI.

How do I change the vibe?

Use the theme dropdown + dark/light button. It saves automatically.
