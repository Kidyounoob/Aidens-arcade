FAQ

Quick answers so the site feels “real”.

Do I need a framework?

No. This base is plain HTML/CSS/JS and already feels modern.

Can I add games later without rewriting this?

Yep. The “Coming soon” cards are the exact layout your games will drop into.

Does theme toggle save?

Yes—localStorage remembers your choice.
